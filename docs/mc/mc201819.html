Geek Christmas Card for 2018-2019

After the big success of the Geek Christmas Card for 2016-2017 I wanted to create a new game for the folks.

Inspired by Tomb of the Mask, this game is about Mike 'the elf'. Mike spend all the year crafting presents for Santa, but while carrying them to the Santa's sleigh. We must help Mike find all his presents!

The game was designed to be played in PC and not in mobile platforms.
