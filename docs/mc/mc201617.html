Geek Christmas Card for 2016-2017

For the Geek Christmas Card 2016-2017 I wanted to make the receiver of the card participant of the joy of creating this special card.

To do so, I designed a game where the player has to help Mr. Snowman, a small and blue snowman, to find his lost hat in seven different levels.

While small, this game is inspired in the end-less games that were popular at the time like FlappyBird, making the player decide the first direction of Mr. Snowman and then only controlling its ability to jump. The game was designed to be played from PC and not from mobile platforms.
